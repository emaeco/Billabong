Setting a benchmark in child-friendly education

BHIS Bhopal commits itself to an unconventional schooling approach that caters to each student as a unique individual aspiring to learn, experience, innovate, grow, and transform into a confident, well-rounded, global citizen. Established in the year 2005, BHIS Bhopal is a part of Kangaroo Kids Education Limited (KKEL), built on the concept of ‘Experiential Learning’ that offers a stress-free learning ecosystem to over 130+ schools, 55 cities, 5 countries, 200000+ students. With a determination to raise the bar in quality education, BHIS Bhopal’s high-calibre academic educators engage students in innovative, activity-based learning models that facilitate practical learning and a thorough understanding of subjects. As an educational institute, we believe in multifaceted knowledge that guides each child to find their innate talent, their purpose, their own North Star.
